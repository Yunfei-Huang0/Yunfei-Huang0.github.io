About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Helmholtz-Zentrum Hereon in Geesthacht, working on data-driven modeling for the Earth system. Previously, I obtained PhD in biophysics at the University of Cologne on advanced data analysis for traction force microscopy and data-driven discovery of physical equations.

I am interested in the uncertainty quantification for the inverse ill-posed problem, e.g. the image reconstruction and the identification of the governing equations (ODEs, PDEs and SDEs), by using deep learning, Bayesian approaches, and information theory.

Projects

EURO Meets NeurIPS 2022 Vehicle Routing Competition

I was the initiator and lead organizer of the EURO Meets NeurIPS 2022 Vehicle Routing Competition which brought together researchers from the Operations Research (OR) and Machine Learning (ML) community to solve a static and dynamic variant of the Vehicle Routing Problem with Time Windows (VRPTW).

IlikeCLR

IlikeCLR allows to browse and like papers from ICLR 2020 to get instant (maximum ‘like’-lihood) recommendations.

SET® Finder

SET® Finder is an Android/iOS app that uses TensorFlow to run a Deep Neural Network on-device to find SETs in the SET® card game.
