About me

I want to make things better. In other words, I optimize.

Currently, I am a third year PhD student at the Amsterdam Machine Learning Lab (AMLab), supervised by Herke van Hoof and Max Welling. My research focuses on using (Deep) Reinforcement Learning to learn algorithms capable of solving discrete optimization problems.
